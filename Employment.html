We at The Arc are fortunate to work with some of the greatest people and families that you will ever meet. We are looking for employees who are interested in providing quality supports to this amazing group of people. Complete an application today!

Full and Part-Time Mentors

Full and Part-time Mentors needed to support people who experience disabilities in the Clarion County and Venango County areas. Mentors are responsible for implementing program plans and working cooperatively with families, interdisciplinary teams, and outside agencies. Must be committed to providing quality supports. Must be flexible. Days and hours will vary. Must be 21 years of age or older, possess a valid driver's license, have a high school diploma and acceptable clearances.

For more information: Call 814-226-7033 Ext 302 to schedule an interview. Applications can also be obtained at The Arc office locations in Clarion and Seneca.
